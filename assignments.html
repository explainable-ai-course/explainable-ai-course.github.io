Each group is expected to thoroughly review their assigned research paper and give a 15-20-minute presentation on its objectives, methods, key findings, and a critical evaluation of its strengths and weaknesses. After the presentation, a 5-10-minute discussion will take place to explore the results, possible improvements, and broader implications of the research.
